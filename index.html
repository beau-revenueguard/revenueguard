Ask about the pilot How it works

A focused recovery process

The pilot turns existing ServiceTitan reports into a prioritized list of unresolved membership balances for your office team to review.

01

Provide three exports

Send recurring billing history, customer membership, and open invoice reports. No account credentials are required.

02

Identify unresolved balances

We remove payments already resolved, credited, refunded, or tied to inactive memberships.

03

Work the recovery queue

Your office receives a prioritized customer list showing the amount requiring review and the recommended next action.

Founding pilot

We are inviting three to five residential HVAC companies using ServiceTitan to participate in a free four-week pilot.

  • One initial membership-payment analysis
  • Prioritized customer-level recovery list
  • Total unresolved balance requiring review
  • Four-week recovery measurement period
  • No software installation or ServiceTitan login access
  • No obligation to purchase anything afterward

Your data stays under your control

RevenueGuard accepts only the reports required for the analysis. We do not request card numbers, banking information, payment credentials, ServiceTitan passwords, or API credentials.

Participant data is used only for the agreed pilot analysis and is deleted after the agreed retention period. Identifiable information is never entered into public AI tools or sold to third parties.

About RevenueGuard

RevenueGuard is an independent software research project focused on improving revenue assurance for recurring-service businesses. We are currently validating a focused membership-recovery process with a limited number of residential HVAC companies using ServiceTitan.
