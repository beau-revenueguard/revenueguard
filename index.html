Frequently asked questions

The pilot is designed to be simple, limited in scope, and low-risk for participating companies.

Do you need access to our ServiceTitan account?

No. RevenueGuard works from reports your team exports. We do not request passwords, login access, or API credentials.

Do you need payment information?

No. We never request payment credentials, card numbers, banking information, or other sensitive payment details.

How long does the pilot take?

The initial analysis begins after the required reports are received. Recovery results are then measured over four weeks.

Is there any obligation afterward?

No. The pilot is free, and there is no obligation to purchase software or continue with any paid service afterward.
